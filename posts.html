How I Learned To Stopped Worrying And Love The Bomb

A retelling of my haphazard journey in learning to develop alongside our new agent overlords.

Every word of this series was written by me, a real human boy, and not an AI.

I am being paid to vibecode a greenfield ERP at a $2 million dollar eCommerce business unit at a $35 million dollar company. I think that is wild. My friends agreed, and they asked me to blog about my learnings and experiences.

The purpose of this blog series is to share, as objectively as possible, my journey into collaboratively building an entire ERP at a scaling business with a small team. I am going to share the problems I am trying to solve, my approach, the tools we use, and report what worked for us and what didn't.

I'm actually having a really great time learning new engineering paradigms, and even though I'm not quite in the code anymore, I have rediscovered my love of building something tangible that makes people's lives easier.
