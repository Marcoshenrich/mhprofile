Streetcat Snap

In early 2026 I moved to a street in Oakland, CA which has several roaming house and street cats. I am playing Pokemon Snap with them, and am featuring only the highest rated picture of each one.

Dexter

Dexter is the king of the street. He is a big goofy lad and is very friendly. I called him Baxter before learning his name, and afterwards I felt very smart.

Sassy

I've seen Sassy exactly one time and she totally snubbed me. Just walked right on by.

Casper

Casper likes to grab a leaf and sit in the middle of the street and meow loudly. He is very proud of his leaves.

Odo

Beautiful eyes with a racoon's tail. Doesn't hang around much.

Shadow

A shy boy. What a cutie.

Rascal

Scarey looking and very skittish.

Princess

Truly a majestic beast. Extremely shy.

Kitten of Deceit

This is a silhouette cutout of a cat. At first I thought it was a real cat, but then I found out I was being lied to. Life can be cruel like that.
